The dispute had been ongoing for months, with both sides presenting their cases to FIFA.

## FIFA's Ruling
After careful consideration, FIFA has rejected Belgium's request, citing Article 8 of the FIFA Regulations on the Status and Transfer of Players. In Balogun's case, FIFA determined that he had indeed represented Nigeria in international competitions and was therefore bound to play for them. The ruling is a significant victory for Nigeria, who can now count on Balogun's services in upcoming matches.
