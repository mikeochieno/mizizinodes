The earthquake that struck Venezuela was a powerful 7.3-magnitude quake that caused widespread destruction and chaos. The rescue efforts have been challenging, with many buildings reduced to rubble and debris scattered everywhere. However, despite the challenges, the rescue workers have been able to pull out many people alive, including the two boys who were rescued over the weekend.
