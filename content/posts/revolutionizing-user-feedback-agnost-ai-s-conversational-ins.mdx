## Context: The Problem of User Feedback
User feedback is essential for improving conversational AI systems. However, extracting feedback from conversations can be a daunting task. Traditional methods rely on explicit feedback mechanisms, such as surveys or rating systems, which can be intrusive and often yield low response rates. Moreover, these methods may not capture the nuances of user interactions, leading to incomplete or inaccurate feedback. Agnost AI's solution aims to overcome these limitations by analyzing conversational data to extract implicit feedback.
